# Objective

Produce a comprehensive research paper on Marcus Vipsanius Agrippa — the Roman general,
statesman, and close ally of Augustus Caesar — covering his military career, political
role, civic contributions, and lasting historical significance.

# Scope

- Biographical coverage from birth (~63 BC) through death (12 BC)
- Military record: the civil wars, naval campaigns, and frontier campaigns
- Political role under the Principate
- Infrastructure and civic works: the Pantheon, aqueducts, and urban development
- Personal relationships: his marriages, his bond with Augustus and Agrippa's sons as heirs
- Historical legacy and influence on Roman imperial administration

# Audience

Readers with general historical interest who want a substantive, accurate account —
not a casual overview, not an academic dissertation.

# Constraints

- Do not present speculation as established fact; mark uncertain points clearly
- Cite ancient sources (Suetonius, Cassius Dio, Velleius Paterculus) where relevant
- Keep tone authoritative but accessible
