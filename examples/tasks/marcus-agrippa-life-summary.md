# Objective

Provide a chronologically ordered summary of the life of Marcus Vipsanius Agrippa,
covering all major phases from his origins through his death and posthumous legacy.

# Requirements

- When and where was he born; what is known of his early life and background
- How he met Octavian (later Augustus) and rose to prominence
- His role in the civil wars: Philippi, Naulochus, Actium
- His administrative and civic achievements during the Augustan peace
- His family life: marriages to Caecilia Attica, Marcella, and Julia; his children
- His death in 12 BC and what it meant for the succession
- His lasting legacy in Roman history and infrastructure

# Sources

- Primary ancient sources: Cassius Dio, Velleius Paterculus, Pliny the Elder
- Secondary: any available scholarly overview of the Augustan period
