# Objective

Write a substantive book review of Edward Gibbon's "The History of the Decline and Fall
of the Roman Empire."

# Requirements

- Summarize the scope and argument of the work: what Gibbon set out to explain and how
- Evaluate Gibbon's thesis about the causes of Rome's decline
- Assess the strengths: prose quality, breadth, the treatment of primary sources
- Assess the weaknesses: historiographical biases, dated scholarship, the treatment
  of Christianity and Islam, gaps in coverage
- Place the work in its historical context: what was understood in the 18th century
  vs. what modern scholarship has revised
- Conclude with a recommendation: who should read this, and why — or why not

# Tone

Critical and substantive. This is a review for a reader deciding whether to commit
to a very long book. Be honest about its demands and its rewards.
