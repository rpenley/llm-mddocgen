# Objective

Produce a structured list of famous Roman statesmen and their primary accomplishments,
spanning the Republic and Imperial periods.

# Requirements

- Include at least twelve figures spanning the Republic and early Empire
- For each person include: name, approximate dates active, role or title, and their
  two or three most significant accomplishments
- Note the historical period (Republic, Late Republic, Principate, etc.)
- Order the list chronologically by period of peak activity
- Where relevant, note how the figure shaped Roman law, governance, or military strategy

# Coverage should include figures such as

- Cincinnatus, Scipio Africanus, Tiberius and Gaius Gracchus, Gaius Marius, Sulla,
  Pompey, Julius Caesar, Cicero, Augustus, Agrippa, Tiberius, Hadrian, Marcus Aurelius
- Do not limit to this list; include others if they are more significant than listed names

# Format

A clean structured list with a brief paragraph per figure — not a wall of prose.
