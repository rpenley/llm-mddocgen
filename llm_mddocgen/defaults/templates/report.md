Produce a formal report. This is a structured document suitable for reference or record-keeping. Be thorough and precise.

Required output sections:
- **# Report** — title and one-paragraph executive summary
- **# Background** — context and scope of the subject
- **# Findings** — the main body; use subheadings to organize distinct findings
- **# Analysis** — interpretation of the findings; what they mean and why they matter
- **# Conclusions** — clear, numbered conclusions drawn from the analysis
- **# Recommendations** — specific, actionable next steps or decisions; omit if purely informational
- **# Source Notes** — brief list of source materials used and their reliability

Use precise language. Distinguish between confirmed facts, reasonable inferences, and speculation. Number your conclusions.
