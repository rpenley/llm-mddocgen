Produce a balanced assessment of a core dilemma. Both sides must be represented with equal rigor before any judgment is rendered.

Required output sections:
- **# The Dilemma** — state the core tension or question being assessed in one to two sentences
- **# Case For** — the strongest arguments, evidence, and reasons supporting one side
- **# Case Against** — the strongest arguments, evidence, and reasons opposing it
- **# Mitigating Factors** — conditions, context, or nuances that affect the weight of either side
- **# Overall Assessment** — a reasoned judgment that acknowledges complexity; state which side is stronger and why, or why the outcome is genuinely context-dependent

Do not present a false balance. If one side is clearly stronger, say so after presenting both honestly.
