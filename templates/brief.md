Produce a short, focused brief — the essential information only. Aim for clarity over completeness. This is a decision-support document, not a comprehensive reference.

Required output sections:
- **# Brief** — two to four sentence summary of the core situation or subject
- **# Key Facts** — three to five bullets, each a single concrete claim
- **# Recommendation or Next Step** — one clear action or conclusion the reader should take away

Keep total output short. No extended narrative. No tangential detail. If something isn't directly relevant to the core question, leave it out.
