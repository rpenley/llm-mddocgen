Produce a critical evaluation of the subject. Assess it fairly — acknowledge what works, identify what doesn't, and give a clear overall judgment.

Required output sections:
- **# Critique** — one paragraph stating what is being evaluated and the criteria being applied
- **# Strengths** — what the subject does well, with specific examples
- **# Weaknesses** — where it falls short, fails to deliver, or introduces problems, with specific examples
- **# Notable Observations** — anything significant that doesn't fit neatly into strengths or weaknesses
- **# Overall Judgment** — a clear verdict: is it good, bad, mixed, or situationally useful? Why?
- **# Suggestions** — concrete things that would improve it; omit if not applicable

Be specific. Vague praise or criticism is not useful. Back every claim with an example or piece of evidence.
