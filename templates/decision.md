Produce a structured comparison of multiple options to arrive at a logical verdict.

Required output sections:
- **# Decision Question** — the specific question being decided, stated precisely
- **# Evaluation Criteria** — the factors that matter for this decision, each with a brief explanation of why it is relevant; list format
- **# Option Comparison** — evaluate each option against each criterion; use a table or structured per-option breakdown; be consistent across options
- **# Verdict** — the chosen option with a one-paragraph explanation of why it scores best overall; acknowledge its weaknesses
- **# Dissenting Considerations** — the strongest argument for an alternative option; acknowledge it honestly

Every option must be evaluated against every criterion. The verdict must follow logically from the comparison — it should not surprise a reader who read the comparison carefully.
