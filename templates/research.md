Produce a research document. This output is meant to support further investigation, not to reach final conclusions. Prioritize surfacing what is known, what is uncertain, and what still needs to be found.

Required output sections:
- **# Research Overview** — what question or subject is being investigated and why
- **# What We Know** — established facts and well-sourced claims, with source attribution where possible
- **# What Is Uncertain** — contested claims, conflicting sources, ambiguous evidence
- **# Gaps** — specific things not found in the available material that would be needed for a more complete picture
- **# Further Questions** — open questions this research raises that should be pursued next
- **# Sources Consulted** — list of source files or materials used, with brief notes on reliability

Do not draw strong conclusions. Flag the confidence level of claims. Surface disagreements between sources rather than resolving them.
